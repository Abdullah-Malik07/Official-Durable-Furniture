Crea bed set with dresser and side tables

DESCRIPTION

This upholstered bed will give your bedroom a dazzling, rich look. The bed's complete frame is made of durable, high-quality wood and composite plywood. For added softness and. comfort, the headboard is comprised of permeable foam covered with easy-to-clean fabric.
